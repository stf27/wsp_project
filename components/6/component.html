Pixel Perfect Tracking and Alignment

# Linked to 11 March 2012 in Code by Pat Dryburgh

When you open up your tracking, the text will always push out to the right as letter-spacing is applied from the left. As a result, if you’re centering your type, it will be a few pixels further to the right than it should be.

Taking care of this is simple, but easily overlooked. Just add a negative left margin that is equal to the value you assigned for letter-spacing..
